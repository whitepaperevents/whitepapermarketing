The Secret to Sales Success

What's the secret that separates superstars from everyone else, and why do they consistently outperform other salespeople? Fanatical prospecting.

Superstars are relentless, unstoppable prospectors. They are obsessive about keeping their pipeline full of qualified prospects. They prospect anywhere and anytime—constantly turning over rocks looking for their next opportunity. They prospect day and night—unstoppable and always on. Fanatical!

Superstars view prospecting as a way of life. They prospect with single-minded focus, worrying little about what other people think of them. They enthusiastically dive into prospecting, asking for referrals, knocking on doors, following up on leads, and striking up conversations with strangers.


We get it. If this sounds intense, don't worry this is what we do best!


  • We don't make excuses: “Oh, this is not a good time.”
  • We don't complain: “Nobody is replying me back.” We test rinse and repeat.
  • We don't whine: “The leads are bad.” We look for better lead sources.
  • We don't live in fear: “What if she says no?” Or “What if this is a bad time?”
  • We don't procrastinate: “I don't have time right now. I'll catch up tomorrow.”
  • We prospect when times are good cause they know there will be another rainy day.
  • We prospect when times are bad because they know that fanatical prospecting is the key to survival.
